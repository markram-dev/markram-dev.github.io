Project Information

  • Project Name: Legendary Remastered (CS:GO Fragmovie)
  • Client: YouTube Channel (Self)
  • Project Date: November 2019 - March 2020
  • Project URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PFdKfGTGYVg

Pushing to new heights

In 2017, I created a montage named "Legendary" to showcase my best moments playing Counter Strike: Global Offensive. It was actually very low quality with minimal effort put towards the editing. Equipped with new software, new skills and new motivation, I decided to remaster my old video into what would become the biggest project I've pursued in my life (so far).

For some time, I strongly considered dropping the design of the original version and creating a completely brand new movie. I decided to maintain the general structure of the original and just touch it up like the restoration of historic artworks. I extended the movie with a cinematic intro, 3 extra minutes of new clips, and outro credits which were mainly experimental editing showcases.

Before I could even start editing, I had to re-record everything at a higher bitrate and texture quality while making small quality-of-life adjustments (i.e: silencing the teammate radio, filtered killfeed, bluer sky!). Use the slider below to see the drastic difference between the old and new recordings:

After rendering all the clips at 240 frames-per-second with intense motion blur, my clips were finally ready to be compiled. Much of my time editing actually involved learning on-the-fly especially with Final Cut Pro visual effects and Motion 5 animations. The editing phase was completed 4 months later.

While I wish I could have done more to make the video not as long and a bit more exciting, I wanted to wrap the project up instead of constantly picking away at something that I'd never be fully satisfied with. Considering the amount of effort, time and new features that were incorporated into the final product, I am very happy with the results.

"Legendary Remastered" is available to watch here.
